Covid Situation and how we handle it

General Guidelines

We are taking the virus seriously and taking stronger precautions than the legal minimum rules
People who aren't taking COVID seriously aren't welcome since they endanger everyone and the event row itself

Masks

The face mask has to be worn at all times
The mask has to cover mouth and nose
Exceptions:
Eating: Sitting on an empty table and avoid communication
Drinking: Turning away from the table while taking the sip

ATK - Antigen Test Kit

An ATK test not older than 24 hours has to be presented at the event
It is not required to have an test from an official doctor. A cheap supermarket one is acceptable

Hand sanitizer

It is advisable to bring hand sanitizer with yourself and use and share it with others since we are touching a lot of game pieces with our hands

Vaccinations

We strongly advise everyone to be vaccinated accordingly
We won't check the vaccination status since it's almost impossible to enter the country without vaccination anyway
